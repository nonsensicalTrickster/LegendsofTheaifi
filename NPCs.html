Wurmhornne

The people of Wurmhornne are typically Elven, with the Deepelves living in the Veins and the rest living above ground. Outside of the Elven people, there are many other fey-adjacent races such as Faeries, Sprites, Archfey, Leshy and many others.

Tinbach

The people of Tinbach are a hardy breed, mostly made up of Dwarves and Orcs. The occasional Halfling can also be found here, though many are so thoroughly interbred between their own race and the Dwarves that they are considered a subrace of their own: Mountainkin. Beyond the Blade of the Frozen can be found a rather impressive span of Dragonborn who populate much of the north-western parts of Tinbach.

Mikahn

The most diverse of the nations in terms of races, Mikahn finds itself home to a plethora of humans, Tabaxi, Nagaji, Halflings, Gnomes, and many, many more. The most predominant race is Beastkin, with Tabaxi being the highest population, but this is only by a small margin.
